
# NurseryNet: Complete Feature List for Investors

This document outlines every feature and page available within the NurseryNet ecosystem, categorized by the value they provide to our key user segments.

---

### For Preschool Owners & Teachers (The "Business-in-a-Box" Suite)

*   **Core Platform & Lead Generation**
    *   **Public Profile Page:** A customizable, SEO-friendly landing page for each preschool, featuring a logo, image gallery, video tours, description, philosophy, features, contact info, and location.
    *   **Profile Preview:** Owners can see exactly how their profile will look to parents before publishing.
    *   **Inquiry Management System:** A dedicated inbox on the owner dashboard to receive and manage direct messages from interested parents.
    *   **"Story" Feature:** Owners can post 24-hour expiring stories (images/updates) to their profile, boosting engagement.

*   **AI-Powered Growth & Branding Tools (The "Creative Canvas" & "Growth Navigator" Tiers)**
    *   **AI Logo Generator:** Instantly create a modern, professional logo for the preschool just by providing a name.
    *   **AI Logo Animator:** A service where owners can upload their existing logo to have it professionally animated by the NurseryNet team.
    *   **AI Social Media Ad Generator:** Upload a photo, choose a marketing goal (Awareness, Engagement, Enrollment), and receive AI-generated ad copy and hashtags ready for Facebook or Instagram.
    *   **AI Funding Hub:**
        *   **Funding Finder:** An AI agent that searches for relevant government, private, and international grants based on the school's needs and location.
        *   **AI Business Plan Generator:** Automatically generates a structured, professional business plan suitable for funding applications based on school details.
        *   **AI Funding Proposal Writer:** After finding a grant, the AI generates a tailored, persuasive funding proposal targeting the specific grant provider.

*   **AI-Powered Classroom & Admin Tools (The "Growth Navigator" Tier)**
    *   **AI Quarterly Report Card Generator:** The AI synthesizes data from graded worksheets to automatically generate a comprehensive, professionally formatted, and branded quarterly report card, complete with a heartfelt teacher's comment.

*   **Dashboard & Analytics**
    *   **Preschool Owner Dashboard:** A central hub to manage the school's profile, view analytics, and access all tools.
    *   **Advanced Analytics:** Tracks key metrics like profile views, leads generated, and engagement rate over time.
    *   **Weekly WhatsApp Reports:** Performance summaries delivered directly to the owner's WhatsApp number.

*   **Resource Library**
    *   **The ECD Excellence Toolkit (Growth Kit):** A free library of downloadable PDF templates for admin and operations, including parent-teacher meeting notes, lesson plan templates, attendance sheets, and more. Premium tiers unlock curriculum-aligned worksheets.

### For Parents

*   **School Discovery & Connection**
    *   **National Preschool Directory:** A comprehensive, searchable directory of preschools across South Africa.
    *   **Detailed Public Profiles:** View in-depth information, photos, videos, features, and philosophy for each school to make an informed decision.
    *   **Direct Inquiry Form:** Securely send messages directly to preschools from their profile page.
    *   **The NurseryNet Excellence Rankings:** A data-driven ranking page to discover the top-performing preschools on the platform.
    *   **Parent Dashboard:** A central hub for parents to manage their activity on the platform.

*   **Premium Features & Peace of Mind**
